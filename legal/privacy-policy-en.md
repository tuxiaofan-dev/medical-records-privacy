# Privacy Policy - Health Records

**Effective Date:** June 29, 2026  
**Version:** 1.0

---

## 🔒 Our Core Commitment

Your health data is stored **entirely on your device**. We do not collect, upload, or share any personal information.

---

## 1. Overview

Welcome to "Health Records" (hereinafter referred to as "this app"). We understand the sensitivity of health data and are committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

## 2. Data Collection

### 2.1 We Do Not Collect Any Data

This app uses a **pure local storage** architecture:

- ✅ All health data is stored only on your device
- ✅ No data is uploaded to cloud servers
- ✅ No personal information is collected
- ✅ No third-party analytics tools are used
- ✅ No advertisements are displayed
- ✅ Your usage behavior is not tracked

### 2.2 Permission Usage

**📷 Camera Permission**
- Purpose: Take photos of medical reports
- Note: Photos are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to any server

**🖼️ Photo Library Permission**
- Purpose: Import existing medical report photos from your library
- Note: Only accessed when you actively select photos

**🔔 Notification Permission**
- Purpose: Medication reminders (if you set them)
- Note: Only used when you enable reminder features

## 3. Data Storage

All data is stored on your device using the **Hive local database**, including:

- Health records (blood pressure, blood sugar, and other values)
- Captured photos
- App settings (theme preferences, etc.)

**⚠️ How to Clear Data:**
- Tap "Clear Data" in the app settings page
- Uninstall this app

## 4. Third-Party Services

This app **does not use** any third-party services:

- ❌ No third-party SDKs
- ❌ No third-party analytics
- ❌ No third-party advertisements
- ❌ No third-party data storage

## 5. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and discover that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete the relevant data.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Updated policies will be announced within the app, and we recommend that you review them regularly.
